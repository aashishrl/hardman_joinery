HARDMAN Joinery has the capacity and experience to undertake any sized Heritage or Colonial-style restorations or original construction.

From individual or large scale domestic and residential properties to major restoration of public buildings, galleries, libraries, historic homes and offices.

We design, manufacture, deliver and install.

Heritage Profiles

We've built an extensive collection of Heritage Cutters, giving us the ability to match or create a large array of different milled profiles

These profiles can be used for

  • skirting boards
  • picture rails
  • door jambs
  • architraves
  • lintels
  • window frames
  • window sills
  • hand rails
  • furniture facings
  • drawers
  • cupboard doors
  • decorative fireplaces
  • staircases
  • general mouldings
  • inserts
  • panel beading
  • as well as all other custom requirements.

From major Heritage building restorations, to private dwellings or small offices, HARDMAN Joinery can add dignity and old-world charm
to any project
